About Us

The story behind Shantee's Beauty

Meet Shantee

Shantee is a professional beauty artist specializing in lashes, makeup and nail art. Based in New Brighton, Gqeberha in the Eastern Cape, she has built a reputation for delivering stunning, high-quality beauty services to every client.

With a passion for making people feel confident and beautiful, Shantee takes pride in her attention to detail and personalized approach. Every client is treated with care and creativity.

Our Mission

To provide top-quality beauty services that enhance natural beauty and leave every client feeling glamorous, confident and empowered.
